- XHTML is extensible HTML
	- All tags must be writeen in lower case
	- Nested tags must be well formed (ie:<p><b>something</b></p>)
	- tag attributes must be quoted (ie:<table border="2"></table>)
	- tags must always be closed (ie: <br />
